What is Air Quality Index?

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a numerical scale used to communicate how polluted the air is in a specific area. It ranges from 0 to 500, with higher numbers indicating worse air quality. The AQI value is determined by the highest concentrations of six pollutants. This exhibit explains how AQI is computed, and display the AQI data of Atlanta area in 2023. Let’s learn more about Air Quality by understanding and analyzing the AQI trend in Atlanta!
Data source: US Environmental Protection Agency. Air Quality System Data Mart [internet database] available via https://www.epa.gov/outdoor-air-quality-data. Accessed Mar 25th, 2024.
